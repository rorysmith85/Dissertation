Marijuana legalization is gaining steam across the US, and Canada recently legalized it altogether. But legalization is a complicated process, highlighted by the current chaos in California. In a world divided on the war on drugs, the success or failure of legalization in these countries will likey feature prominently in future drug policy.

This series of articles looks at these issues in depth, starting with California's guerilla grows.
